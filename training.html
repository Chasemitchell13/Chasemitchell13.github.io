Warm Up Flow

5 to 8 minutes: light cardio, dynamic mobility, and activation. Move gradually toward the patterns you will use in your session.

Strength Basics

Push, pull, hinge, squat, carry. Keep reps in moderate ranges and leave one or two reps in reserve.

Recovery

Sleep, protein, hydration. A short walk the day after hard sessions often helps more than static stretching alone.
